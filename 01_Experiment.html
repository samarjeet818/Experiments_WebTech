Indian Food

Indian Food and Their Details:

Indian cuisine is one of the most diverse culinary traditions in the world,
known for its rich flavors, aromatic spices, and regional variations that
reflect the country's cultural and geographical diversity.

Regional Varieties:

North Indian Cuisine features popular dishes like Chole Bhature
(chickpeas with fried bread), and various breads like Naan and Paratha.
The region is famous for its tandoori cooking method and rich, creamy gravies.

South Indian Cuisine:

South Indian Cuisine is characterized by rice-based dishes such as Idli 
(steamed rice cakes), Masala Dosa (crispy rice crepes with spiced potato filling),
and sambhar (lentil-based vegetable stew). The cooking style often involves 
steaming and uses coconut, curry leaves, and tamarind as key ingredients.
